Morning Inspiration

The day begins with a fresh cup of coffee and browsing through design inspiration. The morning light streams through the window as ideas start to form for today's creative project.

Class Discussion

In class, we explore Rafael Rozendaal's websites, discussing how color and motion create emotional responses in digital art. The conversation sparks new perspectives on my own work.

Flexbox Exploration

Back at my desk, I dive into CSS Flexbox, experimenting with layouts and responsive designs. The flexibility of this tool opens up new possibilities for my webpage structure.

Storyboarding

With wireframes taking shape, I begin storyboarding my comic project. Each panel represents a moment in the narrative, carefully considering visual flow and user experience.

Scroll Animation

Implementing scroll-triggered animations brings the webpage to life. Elements fade, slide, and transform as the user journeys through the content, creating an immersive storytelling experience.

Evening Reflection

As the day winds down, I review the progress made. The webpage is taking shape, with scroll animations that tell a compelling story of this creative journey.
